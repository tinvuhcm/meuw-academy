# Reports

This folder holds operational reports for this feature.

## What Goes Here

- Post-implementation reports (what was built, what deviated from plan, lessons learned)
- Debugging reports (root cause analysis, investigation findings)
- Performance or quality audit reports
- Any operational document that captures WHAT HAPPENED (not what to do -- that is a plan)

## Naming Convention

Use descriptive names with date stamps: `{topic}-report_{dd-mm-yy}.md`

Examples:
- `migration-report_15-03-26.md`
- `performance-audit-report_22-04-26.md`

## When to Create a Report

- After completing a non-trivial plan (capture deviations and learnings)
- After a debugging session (capture root cause and fix for future reference)
- After an audit or review (capture findings and recommendations)
- When UPDATE PROCESS mode produces actionable insights
